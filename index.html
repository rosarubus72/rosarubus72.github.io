PosterAgent Framework: Generating Academic Posters from Papers

Yibin Yan, Weidi Xie
School of Artificial Intelligence, Shanghai Jiao Tong University
EMNLP 2024 Findings
Paper Code arXiv

Abstract

The paper proposes the PosterAgent framework, a multi-agent pipeline designed to generate high-quality posters from academic papers. It addresses the challenges of long-context and layout-sensitive tasks, leveraging structured parsing, hierarchical planning, and visual feedback. The main contributions include:

  • Introducing a novel benchmark, Paper2Poster, for evaluating poster generation systems.
  • Proposing a 'Top-down' design philosophy that involves global restructuring and local refinements.
  • Developing PosterAgent variants, such as PosterAgent-4o and PosterAgent-Qwen, which show significant improvements in visual quality, text coherence, and overall aesthetic scores compared to existing methods.

Key Technologies
